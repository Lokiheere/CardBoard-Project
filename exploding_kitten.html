Exploding Kittens

Exploding Kittens is cardgame in which players draw cards until someone draws an Exploding Kitten and are out of the game unless they have a defuse. This game is a lot like russian roulette but except the lethal part.

Instruction

Requirements

  • 2-5 players needed

Set Up

  • To start, remove all the Exploding Kittens (4) from the deck and set them aside (Exploding Kittens?)
  • Remove all of the Defuse Cards (6) from the deck and deal 1 to each player (Defuse Cards?)
  • Insert the extra Defuse Card(s) back into the deck
  • Shuffle the deck and deal 7 cards face down to each player. Everyone now has a hand of 8 cards total. (7 cards + 1 defuse).
  • Look at your cards but keep them secert.
  • Insert enough Exploding Kittens back into the deck so that there is 1 fewer than the number of people playing
  • Remove any extra Exploding Kittens from the game
  • Shuffle the deck, and put it face down in the middle of the table (Also make space for a discard pile)
  • Pick a player to go first. (Some sample criteria: most excited to go first, most intimidating odor, shortest spleen, etc)
  • Oh yeah do your turns clockwise...

How to Play?

  • Gather all 8 of your card from your hand by placing it FACE UP on top of the Discard Pile.
  • Follow the instructions on the card (Cards?)
  • Or you can just play no cards
  • End your turn by drawing a card from the top of the Draw Pile into your hand and hoping it's not an Expolding Kitten.
  • (Remember play as many or as few cards as you'd like, then draw a card to end your turn)
  • Play continues clockwise around the table.
  • Eventually every player will expolde expect for one, who wins the game!
  • You won't ever run out of cards in the Draw Pile because you inserted enough Exploding Kittens to kill all but 1 player

Exploding Kitten

  • You must show this card immediately
  • Unless you have a Defuse Card, you're dead
  • Discard all of your cards, including the Exploding Kitten

Defuse Card

  • If you drew an Exploding Kitten, you can play this card instead of dying
  • Place your Defuse Card in the Discard Pile
  • Then take the Exploding Kitten, and without reordering or viewing the other cards, secretly put it back in the Draw Pile anywhere you'd like

Attack Card

  • Do not draw any cards
  • Instead, immediately force the next player to take 2 turns in a row
  • Play then countinues from that player
  • The victem of this card takes a turn as normal but then, when their first turn is over, it's their turn again
  • If the victem of an Attack Card plays an Attack Card on any of their turns
  • The new target must take any remaining turns plus the number of attacks on the Attack Card just played

Favour Card

  • Force any other player to give you 1 card from their hand. They choose which card to give you

See The Future Card

  • Privately view the top 3 cards from the Draw Pile and put them back in the same order
  • Don't show the cards to the other players

Nope Card

  • Stop any action except for an Exploding Kitten or a Defuse Card
  • Imagine that any card beneath a Nope Card never existed
  • A Nope can also be played on another Nope to negate it

Blank Cards

  • Shuffle the Draw Pile thoroughly. (Useful when you know there's an Exploding Kitten coming)
  • Immediately end your turn without drawinf a card
  • If you play a Skip Card as a defense to an Attack Card, it only ends 1 of the 2 turns. 2 Skip Cards would end both turns
  • These cards are powerless on their own, but if you collect any 2 matching Cat Cards
  • You can play them as a Pair to steal a random card from any player
  • They can also be used in Special Combos
