BS

BS is a cardgame of deception with the cards being place face-down. Every player needs to get rid all cards. But there is times where a player lying call "BS" and watch eveything go down.

Instruction

Requirements

  • Playing Cards
  • At least 2 player or more

Set Up

  • Spilt each card evenly to each player of any amount (No Jokers)
  • Any extra cards are put away

    • How to Play?

    • Try to take turns rotationly
    • The first person must put down 1 or more "Ace" facedown so no one sees what it is (Remember put down a card facedown everytime)
    • Every person has to announce what card they placed
    • A person can lie on what card they place
    • If you think someone is lying call BS (BS?)
    • The next person has to do the same thing but 1 up them until they have to rotate back to Ace (1 up them?)
    • This processes repeats until someone runs out of cards

    Calling BS

    • If you think someone is lying call "BS"
    • Flip there card to see if they're lying
    • If the person was lying they have to take the entire card pile
    • If they said the turth you have to take the entire card pile
