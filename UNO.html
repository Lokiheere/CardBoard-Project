UNO

UNO is a popular card game owned by Mattel, where the players has to place down a matching card until one person successfully loses all there cards.

Instruction

Requirements

  • 2-10 players needed

Set Up

  • 7 cards for each players (Now each player has a deck)
  • Remaining cards are put face down and this becomes the Draw Pile
  • Draw a card from the Draw Pile; Place next to it (if black draw again)
  • What you made became the Discard Pile (it's where you place down cards)

How to Play?

  • Players take turns in rotations (Can be left or right)
  • The card must match with the top card from the Discard Pile
  • The card must match at least one of these: Color;Number;Symbol (If Wild read this)
  • When matched place one down
    Or
    if none draw one from the card pile
  • When place second last card say UNO! Before anyone could notice
  • If failed draw 2 cards
  • Once someone gets rid all of their cards they win

Number Cards

  • Cards with numbers like 0-9

Skip Card

  • Skips the next player

Reverse Card

  • Reverse direction of whos next

+2 Card

  • Next person draws 2 cards

Wild Card

  • Changes the color
  • Can be place on any card

+4 card

  • Changes the color
  • Next players has to draw 4 cards
  • Can be place on any card

Blank Cards

  • These cards can be anything
  • For example you can make it a draw 6 or skip all
  • It can be anything really the choice is yours
  • If you find a card called "shuffle" every players deck put them together shuffle them and spilt them up evenly

Alterntive Rules

stacks rules

  • Basicly makes it where you can place more than one card down
  • But it must have the same symbol or number

Stacking +4/+2

  • Like "stacks rules" except you +2 and +4
  • You can stack a +2 with a +4 but you can't stack a +4 with a +2

Draw until place

  • This rule makes it where instead of drawing once
  • You have to keep drawing cards until you draw a card you can place down

Seven-O

  • When somone places down a 7, the player that place down the 7 can switch there hand with someone elses

Jump-In

  • If someone places down a card that has the same color and symbol
  • You can play your card immediately even when it's not your turn
